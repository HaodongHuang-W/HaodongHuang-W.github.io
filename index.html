Junhyeok Ahn

I am a Ph.D. candidate working with Luis Sentis at the University of Texas at Austin. My research interests lie broadly in planning, control, optimization, and machine learning algorithms for legged robots. I strongly believe that extraordinary agility, versatility, and robustness can be achieved when rigorous tools from control theory meet machine learning techniques.
